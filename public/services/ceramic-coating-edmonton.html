Our Ceramic Coating Process

Step 1: Vehicle Inspection & Assessment

We begin with a thorough inspection of your vehicle's paint condition. This helps us identify any scratches, swirl marks, or imperfections that need to be addressed before ceramic coating application.

Step 2: Deep Cleaning & Decontamination

Your vehicle undergoes a comprehensive full body wash, followed by clay bar treatment to remove embedded contaminants from the paint surface. We also perform wheel washing and engine detailing to ensure every part of your vehicle is pristine.

Step 3: Paint Correction & Polishing

Using professional polishing techniques, we correct paint imperfections, remove swirl marks, and perform scratch removal where possible. This step is crucial for achieving a flawless finish before ceramic coating application.

Step 4: Surface Preparation

The paint surface is cleaned with isopropyl alcohol to remove any oils, polishing residue, or contaminants. This ensures maximum bonding of the ceramic coating to your vehicle's paint.

Step 5: Ceramic Coating Application

Our certified technicians apply the ceramic coating panel by panel, ensuring even coverage and proper bonding. This meticulous process requires precision and expertise to achieve optimal results.

Step 6: Curing & Final Inspection

The ceramic coating is allowed to cure properly in a controlled environment. After curing, we perform a final inspection to ensure the coating has bonded correctly and meets our quality standards.
