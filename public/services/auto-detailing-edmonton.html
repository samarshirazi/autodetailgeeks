Our Auto Detailing Process

Step 1: Inspection & Consultation

We begin every appointment with a detailed walk-through, noting paint condition, interior concerns, and your goals. This lets us tailor a plan that delivers the best results for your vehicle.

Step 2: Exterior Wash & Decontamination

A gentle hand wash, wheel cleaning, iron remover, and clay bar treatment remove stubborn contaminants, prepping the surface for polishing and protection.

Step 3: Paint Correction & Polishing

We refine the paint with professional polishers to eliminate swirl marks, oxidation, and light scratches, restoring gloss and clarity before protective coatings are applied.

Step 4: Interior Deep Clean

High-powered vacuuming, steam cleaning, and shampooing reach every surface. Leather conditioning, plastic rejuvenation, and streak-free glass leave the cabin spotless.

Step 5: Protection & Finishing Touches

We apply ceramic coatings, waxes, or sealants for lasting exterior protection and add UV blockers inside to guard against cracking and fading. Tires are dressed, windows polished, and scents added on request.

Step 6: Quality Assurance

A final inspection with you ensures every expectation is met. We review maintenance tips and recommend a detailing schedule to keep your vehicle pristine.

Edmonton Auto Detailing for All Seasons

Edmonton’s extreme weather demands more than a quick rinse at the car wash. Winter road salt accelerates corrosion, freezing temperatures stress rubber seals, and springtime gravel leaves chips and scratches. Summer UV exposure fades paint and dries leather, while autumn debris clogs drains and stains clear coats. Professional detailing builds a protective barrier against each of these threats.

Our detailing packages are engineered specifically for Alberta conditions: decontamination treatments remove salt residue, ceramic coatings shield against UV and chemicals, and interior protectants guard against moisture, mildew, and odors. Regular detailing not only keeps your vehicle beautiful—it preserves its resale value and protects critical finishes from Edmonton’s harsh environment.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I schedule auto detailing in Edmonton?

We recommend a comprehensive detail every 4–6 months for daily drivers, with maintenance washes or mini details in between. Vehicles exposed to gravel roads, construction sites, or winter salt may benefit from quarterly protection.

What’s the difference between a car wash and detailing?

A car wash focuses on surface dirt, while detailing cleans, restores, and protects every inch of your vehicle inside and out. Detailing includes paint correction, steam cleaning, seat shampooing, polishing, and protective coatings for long-term results.

Do you offer mobile auto detailing?

Yes. Our mobile service brings professional detailing directly to your location anywhere in Edmonton, Sherwood Park, St. Albert, and nearby communities. We carry our own supplies and ensure the same meticulous results you’d receive in-shop.
