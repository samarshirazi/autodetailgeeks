Our Auto Detailing Process

Step 1: Initial Inspection & Consultation

We start with a thorough inspection of your vehicle's condition, discussing your specific concerns and goals. This helps us customize our detailing approach to your vehicle's needs.

Step 2: Exterior Washing & Decontamination

Your vehicle receives a full body wash using pH-balanced shampoo, followed by clay bar treatment to remove embedded contaminants, road tar, and industrial fallout that regular washing can't remove.

Step 3: Paint Correction & Polishing

We assess your paint for imperfections like swirl marks, light scratches, and oxidation. Our professional polishing process restores gloss and prepares the surface for protection.

Step 4: Interior Deep Cleaning

Complete auto interior vacuuming is followed by steam cleaning, seat shampooing, and sanitization. We clean every surface from the headliner to the floor mats, removing stains, odors, and allergens.

Step 5: Protection Application

We apply your choice of protection—whether car waxing for short-term gloss or ceramic coating for long-lasting durability. Interior surfaces receive appropriate protectants to prevent UV damage and cracking.

Step 6: Final Touches & Quality Check

Windows are streak-free cleaned, tires are dressed, and we add interior scenting if requested. A final quality inspection ensures every aspect meets our high standards before we present your vehicle.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I get my car professionally detailed in Edmonton?

We recommend professional auto detailing every 3-6 months for most vehicles in Edmonton. However, this can vary based on your driving habits, where you park, and seasonal conditions. Winter months may require more frequent interior cleaning due to salt and moisture, while summer might need additional exterior protection against UV rays. Vehicles exposed to harsh conditions or heavy use may benefit from more frequent detailing.

What's the difference between car washing and auto detailing?

A car wash simply removes surface dirt and grime, while professional auto detailing is a comprehensive process that deep cleans, restores, and protects every part of your vehicle. Detailing includes paint correction, polishing, clay bar treatment, interior steam cleaning, seat shampooing, engine detailing, and protective coating application. Think of washing as maintenance and detailing as complete restoration and protection.
