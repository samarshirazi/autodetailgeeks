Seat Shampooing Process

Step 1: Vacuum & Assess

We vacuum seats, identify stain types, and apply targeted pre-treatments.

Step 2: Steam & Agitate

Steam loosens grime while gentle agitation works cleaner into fabrics without damaging fibers.

Step 3: Hot Water Extraction

High-powered extractors flush and remove contaminants, leaving seats clean and nearly dry.

Step 4: Neutralize & Deodorize

We neutralize detergents, treat lingering odors, and apply optional fabric guards.

Step 5: Final Grooming

Seats are brushed for a uniform finish and mats are reinstalled after drying.

Seat Shampooing FAQs

How long before I can sit on the seats?

Seats typically dry within 2–4 hours. We can provide protective covers if you need to drive immediately.

Will shampooing remove salt stains?

Yes. We use dedicated salt removers to dissolve crusted winter stains and flush them from fabrics.

Can you clean child car seats?

We can deep clean removable covers and sanitize bases. For manufacturer safety, we follow care guidelines for each seat.
