Steam Cleaning Process

Step 1: Vacuum & Prep

Loose debris is removed and surfaces are inspected to target areas needing the most attention.

Step 2: Focused Steam Treatment

Steam wands loosen grime on seats, carpets, and hard surfaces while we gently agitate stains.

Step 3: Extraction & Wipe Down

Moisture and loosened soils are extracted or wiped away to leave surfaces clean and nearly dry.

Step 4: Odor Neutralizing

Enzyme treatments or ozone generators ensure stubborn smells are removed at the source.

Step 5: Protection & Finish

Optional fabric or leather protectants are applied, and the cabin is reassembled with a fresh scent.

Steam Cleaning FAQs

Is steam cleaning safe for leather?

Yes, when paired with proper technique. We follow steam with conditioners to keep leather supple and protected.

How long does the service take?

Most vehicles take 1.5–3 hours depending on soil level. Steam is often added to full interior detailing packages.

Will steam damage electronics?

No. We control steam pressure and avoid prolonged exposure on sensitive components while still sanitizing high-touch areas.
