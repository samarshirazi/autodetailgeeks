Odor Removal Process

Step 1: Inspection & Source Detection

We locate odor sources, moisture issues, or residue build-up and determine the best treatment plan.

Step 2: Deep Cleaning

Steam cleaning, extraction, and enzyme treatments break down odor-causing contaminants.

Step 3: Ozone Treatment

Vehicles are sealed and exposed to controlled ozone levels that destroy odor molecules at their core.

Step 4: Vent & Filter Sanitizing

HVAC systems are sanitized and filters replaced or cleaned to prevent odors from returning.

Step 5: Final Evaluation

We air out the vehicle, verify odor removal, and share maintenance tips to keep the cabin fresh.

Odor Removal FAQs

Is ozone treatment safe?

Yes. Ozone is effective when used properly. We run treatments in an unoccupied vehicle and ventilate fully before return.

How long does the process take?

Most treatments take 2–4 hours depending on odor severity. Vehicles are aired out before pickup.

Will odors come back?

When the source is removed and you follow our care tips, odors should not return. Severe cases may need follow-up for complete elimination.
