Our Restoration Process

Step 1: Prep & Protect

Vehicle is washed around the headlights, panels are masked, and we document the starting condition.

Step 2: Oxidation Removal

Using progressive wet sanding, we safely remove the cloudy layer while keeping the lens temperature controlled.

Step 3: Polishing & Clarity

Machine polishers refine the surface to crystal clarity, restoring light output and lens transparency.

Step 4: UV Protection

We apply a UV-stable clear coat or ceramic sealant to lock in the clarity and block future discoloration.

Step 5: Final Inspection

We compare before-and-after photos, ensure beam alignment is unaffected, and share simple maintenance tips.

Headlight Restoration FAQs

How long do restored headlights stay clear?

With our UV sealant and regular washes, headlights typically stay clear for 18–24 months. Annual maintenance coatings extend protection even longer.

Can you fix cracked or pitted lenses?

Polishing can reduce minor pitting, but cracked lenses require replacement. We’ll assess and recommend the best solution during your appointment.

Will this help me pass an out-of-province inspection?

Yes. Restored headlights improve light output and can help meet inspection requirements related to brightness and clarity.
