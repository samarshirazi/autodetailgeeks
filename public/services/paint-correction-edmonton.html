Paint Correction Workflow

Step 1: Wash & Decontamination

Snow foam, hand wash, iron remover, and clay bar prep the paint for accurate defect assessment.

Step 2: Paint Inspection

We measure paint depth, mark problem areas, and design a correction plan with you.

Step 3: Machine Polishing

Cutting, refining, and jeweling stages eliminate swirls, scratches, and haze.

Step 4: Finishing & Protection

Panels are wiped with prep solution before sealant or ceramic coating is applied for durability.

Step 5: Final Inspection

We examine paint under multiple light sources and review the transformation with you.

Paint Correction FAQs

How much paint correction do I need?

We assess your vehicle in person and suggest the least aggressive process to achieve your goals while preserving clear coat.

Will paint correction remove all scratches?

We can remove light to moderate defects. Deep scratches that penetrate the clear coat may require touch-up or spot repair.

How long does it take?

Single-stage corrections take 4–6 hours, while multi-stage enhancements may require a full day or longer depending on vehicle size.
